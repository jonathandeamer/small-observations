Small·Observations

a notebook of street art, kept loosely, over years

This is where I keep photos I've taken of paintings, stencils, paste-ups, mosaics, and the occasional bit of writing on a wall. Some I noticed in passing. Some I went looking for. A few I can't remember finding at all.

Mostly captured on whichever phone I had at the time, between about 2014 and now. There are over a hundred so far.
